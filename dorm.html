Our Dorm

A Place to Live and Thrive

The Wesley Foundation has a dorm where students choose to live in Christian community. It is not a classic college experience where community centers and activities drive religion. At Wesley, following a Christ-like college experience is at the heart of all that we do.

Our building is close to campus, offers shared spaces for study and fun, and gives students a place to grow spiritually while building meaningful friendships.

Life at The Wesley

From shared meals to late-night conversations, our spaces help students build community and feel at home.

Family Room

A welcoming space where students gather for conversation, relaxing, and shared community life.

Kitchen

A shared kitchen gives students space to cook, eat together, and enjoy the rhythm of everyday life.

Dining Room

Meals and table fellowship help students connect, laugh, pray, and grow together as a community.

Bathrooms

Residential spaces are designed to support daily life with practical shared facilities for students.

Game Room

A space for fun, rest, games, and relaxed community away from the pressure of classes and schedules.

Basement

A flexible gathering area for students to hang out, watch movies, meet, and build friendships.

Outdoor Spaces

Outdoor areas provide space for events, cookouts, relaxation, and connecting with others.

The Roof

A unique community space for gathering, conversation, and enjoying time together near campus.
