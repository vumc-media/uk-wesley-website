our mission

The Wesley Foundation is a Christian ministry that encourages and supports students in the college journey so that they

  • deepen intimacy with Christ
  • become more like Christ
  • form authentic Christian relationships of acceptance and growth
  • are equipped for ministry and leadership
  • have safe fun
  • and impact their world.

A Christian Community Where Students Belong

The Wesley exists to help students grow in faith, build meaningful relationships, and live out the love of Jesus on campus and beyond.
