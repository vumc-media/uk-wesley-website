A Place to Seek God Together

We are a community of people trying to follow Jesus on our campus, in our city, and around the world. We are UK’s only Christian ministry that has a residential ministry, meaning that we are both a campus ministry and a Christian living community.

Campus Ministry

Find a place to worship, pray, serve, ask honest questions, build friendships, and grow as a follower of Jesus.

  • Weekly worship and gatherings
  • Discipleship and leadership development
  • Prayer, service, and community life

Residential Community

Need a place to stay during your college journey? Join a Christian living community near campus where students grow together.

  • Off-campus, next-to-campus housing
  • Shared community and spiritual formation
  • A supportive place to belong during college
