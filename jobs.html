Head Resident – Wesley Foundation at the University of Kentucky

Position Summary

The Head Resident serves as an assistant spiritual and community leader of the Wesley residential ministry. This role cultivates a Christ-centered living environment where students grow in faith, leadership, and community while ensuring the residence hall operates safely, smoothly, and hospitably. The Head Resident oversees daily operations of the building and partners closely with the Campus Director to support the mission of the Wesley Foundation.

Core Responsibilities

Spiritual Leadership & Community Life

  • Foster a Christ-centered culture rooted in humility, integrity, and discipleship.
  • Provide relational care to residents, offering mentorship, prayer, and encouragement.
  • Work with RAs to address roommate conflicts and personal concerns, connecting students to campus/community resources when needed.
  • Encourage healthy, respectful behavior and a positive living environment.

Student Leader Development

  • Recruit, train, support, and supervise Resident Assistants and student leaders.
  • Coach students leading outreach, small groups, and events so they feel equipped and encouraged.
  • Delegate responsibilities and empower residents to serve and take ownership of the community.

Residential Operations & Administration

  • Oversee day-to-day dorm operations including safety, cleanliness, and policy compliance.
  • Manage room assignments, tours, move-in/out processes, keys, and damage reporting.
  • Maintain clear communication with residents and staff.
  • Coordinate building chores and shared responsibilities with residents.
  • Oversee Resident/Non-Resident parking and Game Day parking.

Facilities & Maintenance

  • Communicate with cleaning and maintenance vendors and track repair needs.
  • Order supplies and manage basic inventory, including paper goods and cleaning supplies.
  • Schedule inspections, pest control, and minor maintenance projects.
  • Respond promptly to facility concerns and communicate with appropriate maintenance contacts and leadership.

Hospitality & Partnerships

  • Help create welcoming spaces through attending meals, events, and participating in shared life.
  • Collaborate closely with the Campus Director and Wesley leadership team.

Qualifications

  • Growing Christian faith and commitment to servant leadership.
  • Experience in discipleship, mentoring, or student leadership.
  • Strong relational, organizational, and communication skills.
  • Ability to oversee facilities and logistics responsibly.
  • Self-starter who works independently and as part of a team.
  • Preferred: seminary student or graduate student with a heart for campus ministry.

Compensation & Housing

  • Furnished private bedroom and private bathroom within the Wesley residence community provided at no cost.
  • Utilities and parking included.
  • $10,000 annual stipend.

Work Environment

  • Live-in position within the Wesley residence community.
  • Includes evening and weekend responsibilities consistent with residential ministry.
  • Flexible scheduling with encouraged time off for personal health and Sabbath.
